How It Works

From interest to inbox in minutes

Reachout handles the research so you can focus on making a genuine connection.

1

Build your profile

Chat with our AI or upload your resume. We extract your background, interests, and goals — taking just 2 minutes. This powers every recommendation we make.

2

Discover professors

Browse our curated database of researchers matched to your interests. Add them manually or import a CSV. We automatically fetch their recent papers and lab details.

3

Generate personalised emails

Our AI reads each professor's latest publications and writes a tailored outreach email that references their specific research — not a generic template.

4

Track & send

Review, edit, and send directly from Reachout. Track email opens and replies automatically. We remind you when to follow up so no opportunity slips away.
